This policy describes the browser storage used by Nexlocal and the choices available to you.

1. What cookies and browser storage are

Cookies are small text files a website can ask a browser to store. Websites may also use local storage for similar preference functions. Some storage is essential to provide a requested feature; other storage may support analytics, personalization or advertising and can require consent.

2. What Nexlocal currently uses

Nexlocal uses local browser storage to remember whether you accepted or declined optional cookie use. The key is used only to retain that preference on the device and browser where you made it. Site hosting may also use strictly necessary technical mechanisms for security, traffic delivery and form-session protection.

Current status: no optional analytics or advertising tool is configured on this website. Accepting the banner records your preference but does not, by itself, activate a third-party analytics service.

Preference record

  • Purpose: remember the choice made in the cookie banner.
  • Type: local storage preference.
  • Duration: until you clear site data or reopen and change the choice.

3. Optional tools

If Nexlocal later introduces optional analytics, advertising or similar tools, we will update this policy and the consent mechanism before loading them where consent is required. Those tools may set their own cookies and receive technical information according to their policies.

Embedded or linked third-party websites may use separate storage when you visit them. Nexlocal does not control storage set on an independent website.

4. Managing your choice

Select “Cookie settings” in the footer to reopen the banner. You can accept or decline optional use. Browser controls can also inspect, block or remove cookies and local storage. Blocking strictly necessary storage can affect security or form functions.

Your choice applies to the current browser and device. Clearing site data removes the stored choice, so the banner may appear again.

5. Changes and contact

We may update this policy when our tools or legal requirements change. The date above identifies the current version. Questions may be sent to support@nexlocal.com or Nexlocal Platform LLC, 2450 Lakeside Drive, Suite 210, Austin, TX 78704, United States.
