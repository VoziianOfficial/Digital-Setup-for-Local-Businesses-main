Privacy Policy

Last updated July 20, 2026Nexlocal Platform LLC

This policy explains how information is handled when you browse Nexlocal or submit an inquiry.

Nexlocal is an independent digital setup information and inquiry platform. We do not directly perform every service listed. Submitted requests may be reviewed and, where appropriate, connected with independent service providers or technology specialists. Availability, scope, pricing and delivery terms vary by project, location and provider.

1. Scope and role

This Privacy Policy applies to the Nexlocal website, its inquiry forms and related communications operated by Nexlocal Platform LLC (“Nexlocal,” “we,” “us” or “our”). It does not govern the independent websites, products or practices of providers or technology specialists that may be introduced through the platform.

If you continue with an independent provider, that provider may give you separate privacy information and is responsible for its own processing.

2. Information we collect

Information you provide

Inquiry forms may collect your name, business name, email address, website URL, request type, service interest, business category, current setup, estimated budget, timeline, goals and other details you choose to submit. We do not request payment card data through the general inquiry form.

Technical information

Our hosting environment may automatically receive limited technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, referring page, request time and security logs. A browser-stored preference records whether you accepted or declined optional cookies. No optional analytics tool is currently configured.

Information from others

We may receive information from an independent provider or business partner when necessary to coordinate a requested introduction, respond to an inquiry or administer the platform, subject to applicable obligations.

3. How we use information

  • Review, route and respond to your inquiry.
  • Assess whether an independent provider or technology specialist may be relevant.
  • Coordinate an introduction when appropriate.
  • Operate, secure, troubleshoot and improve the platform.
  • Maintain business records and comply with legal obligations.
  • Send communications you request or otherwise permit; you can ask us to stop non-essential messages.

Where required, we rely on consent, performance of steps requested before a contract, legitimate interests in operating a secure inquiry platform, or compliance with legal obligations.

4. When information may be shared

We may share relevant inquiry information with independent service providers or technology specialists when reviewing or facilitating a potential connection. We may also use vendors that support hosting, email, security and website operations. Those parties receive information for their assigned function and are expected to protect it.

Information may be disclosed when reasonably necessary to comply with law, protect rights or safety, investigate misuse, or support a business transaction. We do not state that inquiry information is sold. We do not publish submitted inquiries.

Cross-border processing

Vendors or providers may process information in countries with different data protection laws. Where applicable, appropriate contractual or legal safeguards should be used.

5. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, objection or withdrawal of consent. These rights can be limited by law and by legitimate recordkeeping needs. Email support@nexlocal.com to make a request. We may need to verify your identity.

You can reopen cookie choices with the “Cookie settings” link in the footer. You can also control browser storage and cookies through browser settings.

Children

Nexlocal is designed for business users and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly seek personal information from children.

6. Security and retention

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures intended to protect submitted information. No transmission or storage method is completely secure, and we cannot promise absolute security.

Information is retained only as long as reasonably needed for inquiry handling, platform administration, dispute resolution and legal obligations. Retention varies according to the type of record and the reason it is held.

External links

The site may link to independent websites. Nexlocal is not responsible for their content, availability or privacy practices.

Changes to this policy

We may revise this policy as the platform or legal requirements change. The date above identifies the current version. Material changes may be highlighted on this page.

7. Contact

Questions or privacy requests may be sent to support@nexlocal.com or mailed to Nexlocal Platform LLC, 2450 Lakeside Drive, Suite 210, Austin, TX 78704, United States.
