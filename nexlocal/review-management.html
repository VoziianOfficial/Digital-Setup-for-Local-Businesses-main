Timing and customer consent

Respect the customer before optimizing the workflow.

Request design should reflect platform policies, relevant rules and the actual customer journey. Participation should never be disguised as mandatory.

  1. 01

    Interaction completed

    Verify that the relevant service or purchase has taken place.

  2. 02

    Appropriate moment

    Avoid interrupting urgent support or an unresolved issue.

  3. 03

    Neutral invitation

    Ask clearly without suggesting a particular sentiment.

  4. 04

    Preference respected

    Do not keep sending requests to someone who has declined.
