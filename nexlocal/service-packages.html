Customization

Define the edges before adding more.

Optional additions are useful only when they support the business context and can work with the rest of the setup.

Additional service categories may be explored when they support a defined step in the customer or internal workflow.

Scope may vary by content volume, locations, services, integrations, review needs and the condition of current systems.

Existing platforms and data may introduce technical or policy constraints that need review before a pathway is agreed.

Appointment rules, service areas, inquiry qualification and consent requirements can change the useful setup.

Availability, pricing, scope and delivery terms depend on project requirements, location and independent provider capacity.
